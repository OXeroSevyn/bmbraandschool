F.A.Qs

Who is this course ideal for?

+
It is perfect for aspiring marketers, entrepreneurs, content creators, and anyone who wants to master digital marketing while running a real business.

What are the core skills taught?

+
The curriculum covers a complete mix of Business Fundamentals, AI Tools, Social Media, Website Development, SEO, Google Ads, CRM, Ecommerce and No-Code Development.

Is prior experience required?

+
No. The training starts from the basics, making it suitable for complete beginners as well as professionals looking to upskill.

Are AI tools included?

+
Yes. The course teaches how to use Generative AI — from content creation and design to workflow automation and AI agents.

What is the final outcome?

+
By the end of 5 months, students will have hands-on experience running campaigns, building websites, and a portfolio ready for job applications or launching their own business.

What certifications are included?

+
The course includes SEMRUSH Certification preparation as part of the SEO module, along with a BraandSchool completion certificate.
